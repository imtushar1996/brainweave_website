About the Video Editor Internship!

Brainweave Video Editor Internship is a virtual internship in which interns are selected which are efficient in video editing and animation. Following are the details for the same-

Internship Type: Work from home (Virtual)
Eligibility:
1. Candidates having good knowledge of Multimedia And Visual Communication
2. Must have experience of Adobe Premier Pro CS 6, Adobe After Effects, etc.
3. Prefer Expertise In Video Editing Tools, Adobe Premier Pro CS 6, Adobe Audition, Adobe After Effects, Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Dreamweaver, Adobe Flash Player, MAYA
Position: Video Editor Intern
Duration: 1 month
Stipend: Rs. 3,000
Skills Required: Adobe Premier Pro CS 6, Adobe After Effects
Benefits:
1. Certificate of Appreciation would be given
2. Recognition of your work & Confidence building.
3. Networking with Entrepreneurs/Professional who can guide you/recommend you.
4. Practical exposure & Development of new skills.
5. Letter of Recommendation by Brainweave for your future endeavours
How to apply?
You need to mail at brainweave.info@gmail.com with subject as "Video Editor Internship Submission" and in content, you need to specify your name, contact no., college/organization name and address. Also, you need to attach two items which are very important for your selection purpose-
=> Your resume
=> A video related to
1. Explaining any type of new technology
2. Related to any brain development activity/exercise
3. Related to any trending topic in field of science and technology
4. Teaser video about Brainweave
5. Any review video on any new gadget or technology
6. Any interesting video giving a feedback about brainweave
7. Any video giving your views on how we can bring a revolution in education
8. Any video related to Vedic Match or mental ability tricks
9. Any interviews taken on any social issue or trending technology
10. Any other videos related to domain Brainweave works in.

Note - Your video should be original, interesting and without any copyright issues. Good quality videos with good editing are preferred. You can make a good animated video either featuring yourself and your friends or making video using any video editor tool. You can download our logo from here. Good enteries will also be uploaded on official facebook page as well as youtube channel of Brainweave, so all the best!
