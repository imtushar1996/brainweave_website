About the Fellowship Program!

Our fellows are the modern youth who can strongly identify and associate with the in adequacy in the current education. The job entails, fellows teaching a class of students of various schools, preferably the school nearby their college/home. The teaching will be completely different from regular schooling. The fellows will be informed beforehand of the entire curriculum of the class. They would have to develop the course materials according to the curriculum and with the help of the guidance from the internship mentor.
