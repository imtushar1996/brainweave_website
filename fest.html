Partner with us!

Brainweave is open to event associations as Education Partner or Outreach/Media Partner or Brain Development Partner. We have sponsored large number of campus festivals as well as various events and competitions held in colleges and schools.A partnership is an ideal platform to further the reach of your event or festival. The partnership gives you on-site branding opportunities, as well as a targeted platform to drive your acquisition campaigns. We believe in promoting and supporting the events and festivals via both online and offline marketing and always try our best to make the association for a lifetime.

Association Benefits

1. Listing you our Partner on all online and offline collaterals related to Brainweave
2. Dedicated link to your company/event website
3. Recognition on all social media platforms where we have our presence
4. Dedicated mass message blasts to college students through our Campus Ambassador Groups
5. Co-promotion on other partner/sister portals
6. Chance for collaborations in future events and campaigns
7. Logo Integration on our website with back-link to your website/website
8. Increased brand awareness by inclusion in pre, during and post event marketing efforts
9. Indirect Monetary Sponsorships (for selected college festivals/events)
