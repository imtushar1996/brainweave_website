About the Workshop Trainer Internship!

Brainweave Workshop Trainer Internship is a client-based internship wherein the trainee will deliver workshops under Brainweave at different schools, colleges and other institutions about his/her expertise domain. The stipend of the internship will be decided on the basis of the workshop footfall. Other perks include a Letter of Recommendation by Brainweave, a certified Brainweave Workshop Trainee as well a pre-placement offer (PPO). A person-to-person interview will be taken as well a demonstration will be required after the interview. English proficiency and communication skills will be checked.
Check this page to know more about the workshops you can apply for as a trainer. Your domain maybe different from those listed there.
