The Vibgyor Mind Challenge 2018

Brainweave presents The Vibgyor Mind Challenge 2018, a annual national competition for college as well as school students which crowd-sources ideas from college students about a pressing society issue and an innovative solution to solve it.
Following are the details for the contest:

Eligibility:
Anyone currently studying in any standard of school or pursuing any college degree or having graduated can apply for the competition.
Entry Fees: Free of Cost
Submission of Enteries Format: Doc/Pdf (minimum 500 words)
Deadline: February 25, 2018 11:59 p.m. IST
What to submit: State a society problem and then give your innovative solution to curb aside that issue
Prize Money: Rs. 10,000/-
Other Perks & Benefits:
1. Certificate of Participation would be given to each participant.
2. Improvement in content writing skills
3. A chance to get funding by Govt. of India if your idea is selected
How to apply?
You need to mail at brainweave.info@gmail.com with subject as "The Vibgyor Mind Challenge 2018 Submission" and in content, you need to specify your name, contact no., school/college/organization name and address. You need to attach a doc/pdf file of your submission in the mail itself with file name as "Your_Full_Name". Late enteries and enteries who will not follow this format scheme won't be entertained.
